The Core Pledge Explained

Understanding Open Libra's Foundation

THE CORE PLEDGE

"We Took Libra from Facebook
To Fulfill the Promise of Good Capital
By Producing the Best Open Asset
Which is Fiercely Independent,
Uses Modern Tools,
Has Plans Measured in Centuries
To Become an Unstoppable Force In Society"

1. Yes, We Took It

We took the Libra vision from Facebook and transformed it into something truly decentralized, independent, and community-owned. What was once a corporate experiment became a movement for financial sovereignty.

2. To Fulfill the Promise of Good Capital

Capital should serve humanity, not exploit it. We're building a financial system that prioritizes long-term value creation, transparency, and positive impact over short-term extraction and profit maximization.

3. By Producing the Best Open Asset

Open Libra represents the pinnacle of open-source financial technology. Every component is transparent, auditable, and community-governed, ensuring the highest standards of security and trustworthiness.

4. Which is Fiercely Independent

Independence from traditional financial institutions, government control, and corporate influence. Our sovereignty is guaranteed by blockchain technology and decentralized governance structures.

5. Uses Modern Tools

Artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and advanced analytics drive our investment strategies. We embrace cutting-edge tools to navigate an increasingly complex and accelerated world.

6. Has Plans Measured in Centuries

Our time horizon spans centuries, not quarters. This long-term perspective enables us to make decisions that create lasting value and weather short-term volatility with strategic patience.

7. To Become an Unstoppable Force

Our ultimate goal is to become an unstoppable force for positive change in society. Through decentralized governance, technological innovation, and century-scale thinking, we're building infrastructure that will outlast empires and serve humanity for generations to come.

Join the Movement

Be part of building the future of decentralized capital. Together, we're creating financial infrastructure that serves humanity for centuries.
